The Mt.Samat cross is the cross located in the Mt. Samat. The Mt. Samat was a natural fortridge for the US x Philippines army against the Japanese forces. The cross is for the memorial for the dead soldiers during the battle of Bataan. This is also another place I've been to in the Philippines.
